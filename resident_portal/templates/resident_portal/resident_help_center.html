Help Center

Answers to your questions about using the portal.

How do I track the status of my case?
You can track your cases by going to the "My Reports" page from the main navigation. You will see a list of all your filed blotters and general reports with their current status. You will also receive email and portal notifications for any important updates.
What do the different case statuses mean?

New: Your case has been received and is waiting for review.

Summon/Mediation/Conciliation: A hearing has been scheduled. Please check the "My Schedules" page for the date and time.

Certified: The case has been resolved or is ready for filing in a higher court.

Dismissed/Withdrawn: The case has been closed.

How can I update my personal information?
Click on your profile icon at the top right of the page, then select "Profile Settings". You can update your contact number, address, and other personal details there.
